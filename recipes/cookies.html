The Undisputed Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This chocolate chip cookie recipe is truly the best. Crisp edges and a soft, chewy inside, you will be making this recipe all the time!

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking Soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate Chips

Cooking Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Beat the butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla together
  3. Mix together the flour, salt, and baking soda.
  4. Gradually beat in the flour mixture into the butter mixture
  5. Slowly fold in the chocolate chips into the batter.
  6. Add the dough onto a prepared baking sheet
  7. Cook in preheated oven until golden brown, about 10 minutes.
